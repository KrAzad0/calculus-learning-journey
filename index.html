Master Calculus in One Year

A comprehensive learning journey from basics to advanced topics

One-Year Learning Roadmap

Months 1-3: Foundations

  • Pre-calculus review (algebra, trigonometry, functions)
  • Limits and continuity
  • Introduction to derivatives
  • Basic differentiation rules

Months 4-6: Differential Calculus

  • Chain rule, product rule, quotient rule
  • Implicit differentiation
  • Related rates and optimization
  • Mean value theorem
  • Curve sketching and analysis

Months 7-9: Integral Calculus

  • Antiderivatives and indefinite integrals
  • Definite integrals and the fundamental theorem
  • Integration techniques (substitution, by parts)
  • Applications: area, volume, work
  • Numerical integration

Months 10-12: Advanced Topics

  • Sequences and series
  • Power series and Taylor series
  • Parametric equations and polar coordinates
  • Multivariable calculus introduction
  • Differential equations basics
